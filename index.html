Asteroids Assault

Objective:

  • Save planet Earth from incoming asteroids by shooting them down with your scope.
  • Your speed is your only defense against the asteroids.
  • Try to achieve the highest score with your 3 lives.

Controls:

  • Use your cursor to target the asteroids with scope.
  • Click over them to destroy.

Scoring:

  • Earn points for each asteroid you successfully destroy.
  • The more asteroids you destroy, the higher your score will be.

Lives:

  • You start with 3 lives.
  • If an asteroid reaches the surface of Earth without being destroyed, you lose 1 life.
  • The game ends when you run out of lives.
